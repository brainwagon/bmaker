### 6. **Poppins + Spectral**
- **Heading:** Poppins (Bold)
- **Body:** Spectral (Regular)

- **Best for:** Marketing, branding agencies

### 7. **Bebas Neue + Open Sans**
- **Heading:** Bebas Neue (Regular)
- **Body:** Open Sans (Regular)

### 8. **Cormorant Garamond + Roboto**

- **Best for:** Law firms, finance

### 9. **Cinzel + Nunito Sans**
- **Heading:** Cinzel (Bold)
- **Body:** Nunito Sans (Regular)
- **Style:** Imperial elegance
- **Best for:** Luxury brands, hospitality

### 10. **EB Garamond + Work Sans**
- **Heading:** EB Garamond (Bold)
- **Body:** Work Sans (Regular)
- **Style:** Timeless professional
- **Best for:** Real estate, corporate

## 📋 Key Design Tips

### ✅ Best Practices
- **Size contrast:** Make headings 2-3x larger than body text
- **Weight variation:** Use bold for names, regular for details
- **Limit fonts:** Stick to 2 fonts maximum
- **Test readability:** Print samples before final production
- **Hierarchy matters:** Name → Title → Contact info

### ⚠️ Things to Avoid
- Script fonts for contact information
- More than 2-3 font weights
- Overly decorative typefaces
- Poor contrast with background colors
